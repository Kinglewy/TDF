Community Commitment

People at the Center of Development

At the heart of TDF is the belief that citizens must drive development — ensuring every voice is heard, every household is included, and every cooperative shapes the future of the Rwenzori region.

Monthly Forums (OSC)

  • District-level Opportunity Strategic Cluster meetings every month
  • Participation from farmers, youth, women, cooperatives & leaders
  • Platforms for dialogue, accountability, and experience sharing
  • Align grassroots priorities with national strategies

Identity Centre & Validation

  • Validation of individuals, cooperatives & organizations
  • Community-driven authenticity and recognition
  • Access to training, funding & innovation hubs
  • Strengthens trust, accountability & transparency

Community-Led Accountability

Ensuring transparency through citizen participation

Citizen Evaluation

Communities assess projects through satisfaction surveys and feedback tools.

Feedback Loops

Continuous feedback ensures transparency and improvement of services.

Trust-Based Validation

Recognition is based on community trust, not just administrative approval.

Citizen-Driven Dialogue

TDF empowers communities to actively shape development through inclusive and evidence-based dialogue.

  • Communities propose and vote on development priorities
  • Participatory grant-making systems
  • Evidence-based decision making through research
  • Inclusive platforms for youth, women, and marginalized groups

Exposure & Experience Sharing

TDF creates opportunities for communities to learn, grow, and innovate through shared experiences and partnerships.
