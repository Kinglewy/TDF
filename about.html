About Toro Development Forum (TDF)

Who We Are

The Toro Development Forum (TDF) is a regional umbrella initiative established as an affiliate of the Uganda Development Forum. Our purpose is to localize national ambitions into regional action, ensuring that the Rwenzori region contributes meaningfully to Uganda’s transformation into a self-sustaining economy.

We are a policy-oriented, evidence-driven platform that engages citizens, investors, and institutions in shared development plans. TDF delivers state-of-the-art local services and last-mile investments to ensure long-term development goals are realized.

Our Vision

“To contribute to Uganda’s transformation into a self-sustaining economy by anchoring tenfold economic growth on agro-industrialisation, tourism, mineral beneficiation, and science, technology, and innovation.”

Our Mission

To engage citizens, investors, and institutions in collaborative action, fostering partnerships and policy alignment for structured regional development.

Our Story

How TDF was established and expanded

March 2026

Consultation with the National Strategic Cluster – Tooro Sub Region and Uganda Development Forum at Mountain of the Moon Hotel, Fort Portal.

April 2026

Formal establishment of TDF as an affiliate of the Uganda Development Forum.

Ongoing Growth

Expansion through Opportunity Strategic Cluster (OSC) meetings across Kyenjojo and the Rwenzori region.

Why the Rwenzori Region Matters

Agriculture

Coffee, cocoa, Hass avocado, macadamia, dairy, and fisheries.

Tourism

Rwenzori Mountains, crater lakes, culture, and eco-tourism.

Minerals

Responsible extraction and beneficiation of resources.

Innovation

Youth-driven hubs for agritech, fintech, and renewable energy.

Guided by Musevenomics

TDF is anchored in Musevenomics — a philosophy of evidence-based dialogue, strategic partnerships, and actionable solutions aligned with Uganda’s Vision 2040.

Global Appeal

TDF welcomes partnerships from across Africa and the world — inviting investors, donors, researchers, and innovators to build a resilient, prosperous, and inclusive Rwenzori region.
