Welcome to TDF

Toro Development Forum (TDF) is the regional engine driving Uganda’s transformation into a self sustaining economy. We connect farmers, entrepreneurs, investors, and institutions to create lasting impact across agro industrialization, tourism, mineral beneficiation, and science, technology, and innovation. From the fertile soils of coffee and cocoa to the breathtaking peaks of the Rwenzori Mountains, our region is rich in resources, culture, and resilience. TDF turns these strengths into opportunities for growth, jobs and sustainable prosperity.

What we Do

Our programs

Agro-Industrial Transformation

This program transforms agriculture into a modern, commercial, and export-driven industry. It strengthens key value chains like coffee, cocoa, avocado, macadamia, dairy, and fisheries while building seed systems, farmer training, and agro-processing hubs. The goal is simple — turn farmers into producers, processors, and exporters who compete in regional and global markets

Read More

Community Governance & Participation

This program puts citizens at the center of development. Through monthly OSC forums, communities actively shape priorities, demand accountability, and participate in decision-making. It ensures development is not imposed but co-created by the people

Read More

Investment & Enterprise Development

This program unlocks investment and grows local enterprise. It connects investors, donors, cooperatives, and SMEs through platforms like the Annual OSC Symposium. It drives funding, partnerships, and business growth across the region..

Read More

Tourism & Cultural Development

This program positions Rwenzori as a world-class tourism destination. It promotes eco-tourism, cultural heritage, and community-based tourism that directly benefits local people while preserving nature and culture.

Read More

Science, Technology & Innovation

This program drives digital transformation and innovation. It builds innovation hubs, supports youth-led tech solutions, and integrates technology into agriculture, finance, and energy for a smarter regional economy.

Read More

Mineral Value Addition & Governance

This program ensures minerals create local wealth, not just raw exports. It promotes responsible mining, local processing, and skills development so communities benefit directly from their natural resources
