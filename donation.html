Donations & Support

Toro Development Forum (TDF)

Support Our Mission

TDF's work in peacebuilding, grassroots validation, opportunity clusters, and wealth creation is sustained through transparent donations and support from partners worldwide. Every contribution strengthens our ability to empower communities, build resilience, and drive transformation in the Rwenzori region.

Why Support TDF?

Community Driven Development

Your support ensures citizens lead the decision making process.

Transparency & Accountability

All donations are documented and reported annually.

Global Impact

Contributions help scale grassroots innovations to regional and international levels.

Peace & Prosperity

Funding supports reconciliation, healing, and sustainable livelihoods.

Ways to Contribute

One-Time Donations

Support specific projects such as seed labs, nurseries, or innovation hubs.

Recurring Support

Monthly or annual contributions to sustain OSC forums and symposiums.

Partnerships

Collaborate with TDF through grants, joint ventures, or technical assistance.

In-Kind Support

Equipment, training, or expertise offered to cooperatives and youth groups.

Transparency & Reporting

TDF publishes annual financial reports detailing how donations are used.

Donors are recognized in the OSC Delegates List and Symposium Reports.

Community satisfaction surveys ensure funds deliver real impact.
