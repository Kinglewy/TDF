Strategic Objectives

Driving Measurable Impact in the Rwenzori Region

TDF is guided by three strategic objectives that ensure focused, measurable, and globally relevant development aligned with Uganda’s Vision 2040.

1. Agro Industrialization

Transforming agriculture into a modern, competitive industry.

What We Do:

  • Seedlings & farmer training
  • Processing hubs for key value chains
  • Support for coffee, cocoa, avocado, dairy & fisheries

Impact: Farmers shift from subsistence to commercial production.

Global Relevance: Export-ready products meeting international standards.

2. Inclusive Governance

Promoting citizen-centered development and dialogue.

What We Do:

  • Monthly OSC forums across districts
  • Community engagement platforms
  • Policy dialogue & accountability mechanisms

Impact: Communities shape decisions and hold leaders accountable.

Global Relevance: A model for participatory governance worldwide.

3. Regional Synergies

Connecting local initiatives to national and global networks.

What We Do:

  • Link projects to PDM, OWC, GROW, YLP, UWEP
  • Build partnerships with NGOs & investors
  • Promote research & knowledge exchange

Impact: Increased opportunities through partnerships and exposure.

Global Relevance: Positions Rwenzori in global development networks.
