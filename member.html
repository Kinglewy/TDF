Tooro Development Forum (TDF)

Identity Centre & Grassroots Validation

The TDF Membership Form is part of the Identity Centre. It validates individuals, cooperatives, and organizations, ensuring grassroots authenticity and accountability. Each applicant provides details that strengthen transparency and inclusivity in the OSC process.

Membership / Validation Form Fields

Personal / Organizational Information
  • Full Name / Organization Name
  • Address (Village, Parish, Sub County, District)
  • Contact Information (Phone, Email)
  • Type of Enterprise
